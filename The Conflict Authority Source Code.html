ABOUT US OUR VISION JOIN THE COMMUNITY
Conflict Authority was founded in 2016 when Chris Link first started competing in MMA. The initial vision of Conflict Authority was to be a gym and management company for MMA fighters and other combat athletes. Over time, Chris ceased competing in MMA and Muay Thai, where the vision for Conflict Authority later changed around his love for martial arts and writing. Conflict Authority is now a blog site focused on MMA and other combat sports, to include information about various martial arts, reviews on relevant combat sports news, as well as reviews on combat sports events. The vision of Conflict Authority is to grow the community of readers and writers interested in MMA and combat sports, and eventually evolve into a large multimedia organization. Conflict Authority may again rise into a management company for combat athletes. If you are interested in having your reviews and blog articles published on Conflict Authority, please email theconflictauthority@gmail.com with a description of who you are, what got you interested in combat sports, if you have any history training and/or competing in combat sports, and a two paragraph writing sample reviewing a recent combat sports event. This writing sample will stricly be used for evaluation purposes. It will not be shared with any third-parties, nor used if you are not selected to be a contributing writer. If you are selected to be a contributing writer, you may choose to have the selection published on the page, or not. It is up to you!
