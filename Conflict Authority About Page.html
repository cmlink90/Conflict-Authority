The Conflict Authority

Conflict Authority was originally founded in 2016 by Chris Link. At that time, Chris was an amatuer MMA and kickboxing competitor, who desired to train fighters and manage fighers careers. Once Chris Link stopped competing in martial arts competitions, Conflict Authority was placed by the wayside. Since then, Chris has continued to train in martial arts and be an avid supporter and fan of martial arts and combat sports across many domains to include mixed martial arts, boxing, professional Karate, kickboxing, Muay Thai, Jui Jitsu, and occasionally watching collegiate wrestling. Due to the continued passion for martial arts and combat sports, and the love of writing and written expression, Chris has decided to transform Conflict Authority into a online blog, where individuals can contribute writing about martial arts in general, upcoming and past combat sports events, and news in the combat sports world.
