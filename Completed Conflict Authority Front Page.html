WELCOME

Welcome to Conflict Authoirty. On this blogsite you will find many different things related to MMA and combat sports in general. There will be reviews on upcoming and past combat sporting events, especially MMA events from UFC, reviews on current events and news in combat sports, and edirotials on information concerning various martial arts.

Conflict Authority was initially founded in 2016 as a combat athlete management and training company. The founder, Chris Link, was competing in MMA and kickboxing at the time. When Chris stopped competing Conflict Authority was put on hold. Conflict Authority has now been revived as a blog site focused on MMA and other combat sports

If you are interested in having your reviews and blog articles published on Conflict Authority, please email theconflictauthority@gmail.com with a description of who you are, what got you interested in combat sports, if you have any history training and/or competing in combat sports, and a two paragraph writing sample reviewing a recent combat sports event. This writing sample will stricly be used for evaluation purposes. It will not be shared with any third-parties, nor used if you are not selected to be a contributing writer. If you are selected to be a contributing writer, you may choose to have the selection published on the page, or not. It is up to you!
